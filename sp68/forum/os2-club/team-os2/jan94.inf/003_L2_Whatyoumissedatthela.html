Party!

We held our first annual Christmas party at the Columbia Holiday Inn Executive Center. There was plenty of food and drinks, Phillip decked out as the jolly old elf, and LOADS of giveaways. OS/2 for Windows, Backmaster, VX-REXX, Simply Money, Stacker for OS/2, WordPerfect Office, and even handmade Christmas ornaments were all given away. Thanks to IBM, MSR Development, Watcom, Computer Associates, Stac, Harvey Summers, and Penny Sheperd (respectfully) for donating the items given away!

We also had a tape of the now famous OS/2 vs. Windows NT shootout held by the Houston Area League of PC users last June. Uncut and unedited, it showed OS/2 blowing NT away in a head to head shootout. Well, almost head to head: NT pretty much only ran a slide show of what it "would" do, while OS/2 actually did it's thing.
