It is hard to find a 16550 (Non A). I was told by National Semiconductor that they did everything they could to get all 16550s back. SIO will detect a 16550 and tell you if you have one. I am told that the 16550 was installed in early PS/2 systems. The 16550 was the first shot at a FIFOed version of the 8250 family from National semiconductor. However, I was told by National Semiconductor that the FIFOs of the 16550 are not reliable and they should not be enabled. SIO will treat a 16550 like a 16450. In this mode, they are reliable. National Semiconductor would not provide me with a specification for the 16550. However, I suspect its maximum baud rate is the same as the 16550A which is 256kb.
