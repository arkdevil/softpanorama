Well, I just got my first PS/2 system (for testing of SIO) and I am using the NeoTech Quad Serial card in that system. What can I say, it works beautifully and interrupt sharing has never been a problem on MCA systems. This card (as shipped) came with four individual 16450 chips installed, but they were in sockets so I installed 16550 UARTs before I installed the card. The card has one large connector on the back of the card and the supplied cable expands that to 4 standard DB-25 connectors.
