I am very impressed with the STB 4COM card, they have almost got it. Yes, its the same company that makes the VGA cards. The 4COM card uses a 16554 which is the equivalent of four 16550A. Comm ports can use IRQs 2/9, 3, 4, 5, 10, 11, 12, and 15. Any or all ports can share IRQs, and they have done this right. I tested SIO with all four ports of the 4COM on IRQ12 and they worked correctly. The user can select any of the following I/O port address for a comm port: 3F8, 2F8, 3E8, 2E8, 1A8, 1E8, 1F8, 2A8.
