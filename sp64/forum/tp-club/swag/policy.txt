SWAG (SourceWare Archival Group) is a collection of source code and program
examples for the PASCAL programming language. The material has been donated
by  various  PASCAL  programmers  from  around  the  world,  who  desire to
contribute to the advancement of  one of the greatest programming languages
there is.  SWAG packets are  available in 56  different categories covering
EVERY aspect of the PASCAL language, and ALL ABSOLUTELY FREE !!!

It  is  the  intention  and  purpose  of  SWAG  to be a learning, teaching,
helping,  tutorial and  support aid  for the  PASCAL programming community.
SWAG does NOT intend to be a distribution channel for shareware programs or
code, but  rather a PASCAL library  of information, code examples  and help
for  beginner and  advanced programmers  alike. NO  COPYRIGHTED MATERIAL IS
PERMITTED  EXCEPT WITH  THE EXPRESS  PERMISSION OF  THE ORIGINAL AUTHOR. We
obtain the  material from the  various PC conference  networks, an we  make
EVERY attempt to make sure that the material contains the contributors name
and any copyright information therein. NO CHANGES ARE MADE TO THE MATERIAL,
AND WE MAKE NO PROMISES OR GUARANTEES WITH RESPECT TO THE MATERIAL IN SWAG.

In the original release, there were  approximately 500 snipets that DID NOT
