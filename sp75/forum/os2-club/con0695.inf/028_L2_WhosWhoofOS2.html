WHO'S WHO OF OS/2

The following list contains the names of the key IBM employees (not contractors) who are intimately involved with the development and marketing of OS/2. These are the "movers & shakers" of IBM who are responsible for the success of the operating system. Their names, titles, locations, and, if known, e-mail addresses are listed. Telephone and fax numbers have not been included in this list.
