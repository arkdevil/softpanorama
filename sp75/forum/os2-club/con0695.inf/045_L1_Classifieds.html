CLASSIFIEDS
OS/2 CONNECT is distributed electronically to a worldwide audience of OS/2 users via computer networks and bulletin boards. Classified advertising is accepted for the following categories:

  • NOTICES

      Legal Notices
      Change in job titles/promotions/transfers
      Meeting Announcements
      Request for Information (RFI)
      Request for Quote (RFQ)
      Request for Proposals (RFP)
      Miscellaneous

    2.
      PRODUCTS, BUY/SELL/SWAP

        Hardware
        Software

      3.
        SERVICES

          Soliciting for a certain type of service (e.g., consulting or teaching assignment) or person/company offering a type of service.

        4.
          EMPLOYMENT OPPORTUNITIES

            Individual Seeking Employment
            Position Announcement

          The price for advertising is $100 (U.S.) per insertion. No discounted rates are available. Checks or money orders may be used for payment, not credit cards (e.g., American Express, Discover, Master Card, Visa). Pertinent sales taxes are the responsibility of the advertiser to assume.

          COMPOSITION:

          Text is limited to 200 words plus one OS/2 bitmap (optional). Offensive language will be edited accordingly. Illegal activities, such as gambling and pornography, are prohibited. Submit your classified in the following format:

          • HEADLINE (please be brief)

          • Classified category (as mentioned above)

          • Text narrative (200 words maximum)

          • Contact information (to be displayed) - e.g., name, address, telephone, fax, E-Mail.

          • Private contact (not to be included in the classified) - Optional; for the OS/2 CONNECT editor to contact you.

          • Indicate which issue(s) of OS/2 CONNECT you wish to run your classified; (e.g., January thru April issues; June only, etc.)

          NOTE: The publisher reserves the right to scale/crop the OS/2 bitmap to suit the publication. Every effort will be made to maintain the integrity of the graphic. If you cannot produce an OS/2 bitmap image, the publisher can convert most graphic files for a setup charge of $10 (U.S.). File formats supported include:

            BMP - Microsoft Windows 3.x Bitmap CUT - Dr. Halo
            DIB - OS/2 or Windows
            EPS - Encapsulated PostScript
            GIF - CompuServe
            IFF - Amiga
            IMG - GEM Paint
            JIF - JPEG - JFIF Compliant
            JPG - JPEG - JFIF Compliant
            LBM - Deluxe Paint
            MAC - MacPaint
            MET - IBM OS/2 Metafile
            MSP - Microsoft Paint
            PCX - Zsoft PC Paintbrush 2.8 and 3.0
            PIC - PC Paint
            PS - PostScript
            RAS - Sun Raster Images
            RLE - CompuServe or Windows
            TGA - Truevision Targa
            TIF - Tagged Image File Format (TIFF) 4.0 and 5.0
            WPG - WordPerfect 5.0 graphic

          OS/2 CONNECT will not take or be responsible for any phone calls or correspondence responding to classifieds.

          Each advertiser submitting a classified is responsible for producing the artwork and text themselves and submitting it to the OS/2 CONNECT editor either on 3.5" diskette or transferring it via BBS or commercial network to the publisher:

          • Publisher's Address & Numbers

          DEADLINES: Classifieds will be accepted up until the second Friday of the prior month (e.g., for the July issue, have your ad in by the second Friday in June).

          For questions regarding classifieds, contact the OS/2 CONNECT editor.
