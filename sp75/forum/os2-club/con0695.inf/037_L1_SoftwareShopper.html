OS/2 SOFTWARE SHOPPER

The purpose of the Software Shopper is to provide a comparative analysis of the prices for the more popular OS/2 software products available through catalog houses. The prices shown in this section is based on the most current printed prices available. They do not necessarily include shipping charges or applicable sales taxes. All prices shown are in U.S. dollars and subject to change without notice. OS/2 CONNECT neither recommends or rejects the catalog houses listed in this section.
