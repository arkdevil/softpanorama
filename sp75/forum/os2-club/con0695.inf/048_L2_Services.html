IBM INDEPENDENT VENDOR LEAGUE

IBM's Independent Vendor League (IVL) supports individuals and companies who develop and market books, newsletters, magazines, training videos, courseware and consulting services for OS/2 and other IBM personal software products. Support comes in the form of access to subject matter experts, early code, up-to-date technical information, and marketing support.

IVL members can submit their products for review and approval, giving them use of the OS/2 Accredited logo in product packaging, advertising and marketing. They have access to the IVL News Service, which distributes their product announcements to trade magazines, newspapers, columnists, bulletin boards, online services, IBM employees, and user groups. IVL companies, members, products and services are also listed in Sources & Solutions, an IBM directory that is shipped with each copy of OS/2 and LAN Server and distributed at trade shows, technical conferences and other events.

IVL membership and services are free. For more information, call Gail Ostrow at (203) 384-9996 or E-mail GailO@Vnet.IBM.COM.
