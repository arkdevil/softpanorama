WHAT'S NEW IN THIS ISSUE?

This section lists all of the changes and additions to OS/2 CONNECT that were implemented since the last issue.

As you browse through this issue you'll notice two new sections: the "Software Shopper" section provides a comparative analysis of the more popular native OS/2 software packages available through the OS/2 software catalog houses, and; "Who's Who of OS/2" (under "Contacts") lists key IBM employees responsible for the development and marketing of OS/2. We hope you will find these new sections useful. Other additions, updates, and deletions include:
