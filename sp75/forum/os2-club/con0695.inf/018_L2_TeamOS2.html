TEAM OS/2 is an international group of OS/2 enthusiasts who volunteer their time and expertise in support of the operating system. They are frequently called upon for assistance at computer trade shows and exhibitions featuring OS/2. Unselfishly, they spend considerable time helping companies and individuals by answering questions about OS/2. They also actively participate in OS/2 testing programs.
