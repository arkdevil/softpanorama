
Microsoft Excel 5.0: HTML Table Converter

Information:   Converts tables in Excel to HTML tables very well. This cuts
work down by a tremendous amount and produces a quality result in very
little time.

A practical model for this process is to maintain an Excel worksheet and
keep that updated and then export it to HTML whenever it is updated.  This
way, a quality product is achieved while maintaining an easy to use editing
environment.

LICENSING:     Freeware; distributed free of charge

REQUIREMENTS:  Excel - if Excel runs on your machine, you can use this.

LOCATION (besides here): VENUS_US
/vol1/dos/internet/html/xls2html/xls2html.xls To use this file, copy it to
the Excel directory, open it in Excel, go to Tools, Macro, click on Install
and then click Run.  A small happy face on the button bar will indicate it
works.  Highlight an area to convert and click the happy face.  It will
guide you through the conversion process.
