What is this newsgroup about? What posts belong here?

comp.infosystems.www.authoring.misc is a forum for the discussion of World Wide Web authoring issues NOT covered in the other authoring newsgroups (see the list below). If your question relates directly to web page authoring and is not covered in this FAQ, a document referenced by this FAQ, or one of the other authoring groups, then it belongs in this newsgroup.

If this group is not appropriate for your question, consider this list of newsgroups in the comp.infosystems.www hierarchy and check out the most appropriate group. If possible, use the most specific group that relates to your topic, rather than a .misc group.

This posting is only an excerpt from the complete WWW FAQ. See the next section for information on accessing the complete FAQ once you have web access.
