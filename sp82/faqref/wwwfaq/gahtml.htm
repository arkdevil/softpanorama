What Is This Newsgroup About? What posts belong here?

comp.infosystems.www.authoring.html is a forum for the discussion of writing HTML. HTML is the simple markup language used to create World Wide Web pages. If your question relates directly to HTML, and is not covered in this FAQ or a document referenced by this FAQ, it belongs in this newsgroup. Note: CGI and image processing issues have their own, separate newsgroups (see below).

If this group is not appropriate for your question, consider this list of newsgroups in the comp.infosystems.www hierarchy and check out the most appropriate group. If possible, use the most specific group that relates to your topic, rather than a .misc group.
