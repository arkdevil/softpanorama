What is this newsgroup about? What posts belong here?

comp.infosystems.www.misc is a forum for the discussion of World Wide Web issues not covered in ANY of the other web-related newsgroups (see the list below). If your question relates directly to the World Wide Web and is not covered in this FAQ, a document referenced by this FAQ, or one of the other web groups, then it belongs in this newsgroup.

If this group is not appropriate for your question, consider this list of newsgroups in the comp.infosystems.www hierarchy and check out the most appropriate group. If possible, use the most specific group that relates to your topic, rather than a .misc group.

  • comp.infosystems.www.authoring.misc
