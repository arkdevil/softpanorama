What special characters are supported in HTML (copyright, accents, etc.)?

It is best to use HTML "entities" whenever possible, such as &lt; for the less-than symbol and &copy; for the copyright symbol. Unfortunately, support for these is not quite universally consistent among browsers. Keep in mind that text-based browsers naturally can't display characters not present in the font being used. When in doubt, spell out "copyright."
