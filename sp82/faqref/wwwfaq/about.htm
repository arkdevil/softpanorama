About the World Wide Web FAQ

The World Wide Web Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) is intended to answer the most common questions about the web.

The FAQ is maintained by by Thomas Boutell <URL:http://www.boutell.com/boutell/>. Copyright 1994, 1995, 1996 by Thomas Boutell and Boutell.Com, Inc.
