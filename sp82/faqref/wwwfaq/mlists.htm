What mailing lists discuss the Web?

There are many mailing lists about the web, and they come and go rather quickly. Please see the W3 Consortium mailing lists <URL:http://www.w3.org/hypertext/WWW/Mail/Lists.html> page and the W3 Consortium's list of other known mailing lists about the web <URL:http://www.w3.org/hypertext/WWW/Mail/Outside_mailing.html> for more information.
