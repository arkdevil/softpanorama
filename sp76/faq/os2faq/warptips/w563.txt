TITLE: 950203 THREE IDE 563 PANASONIC CDROM DRIVES WORKING UNDER
              OS/2 WARP.
 
SYMPTOMS:
    Multiple cdroms connected to the same IDE adapter (SB16 card)
    are not recognize.
 
RESOLUTION:
    Creative Labs has a new driver on their BBS called CD_OS2.EXE.
    BBS number is (405) 742-6660.
    Contact Creative Labs for more information.
    Note that at this point, multiple 563 Panasonic IDE CDROM drives
    are not supported.
