TITLE: 950120 DEVICES WITH NO MIDI SUPPORT UNDER OS/2 WARP 3.0                  
                                                                                
DESCRIPTION OF SYMPTOMS:                                                        
    No midi sound under OS/2 Warp 3.0 MMPM/2 and Winos2.                        
                                                                                
SUMMARY OF RESOLUTION:                                                          
    See details of resolution.                                                  
                                                                                
DETAILS OF RESOLUTION:                                                          
    The following devices that are supported under OS/2 Warp 3.0                
    MMPM/2 and Winos2 will NOT provide MIDI support.                            
    AD1848                                                                      
    Compaq Business Audio                                                       
    Crystal Semi CS4231                                                         
    Toshiba T6600c, T4700c, and T4800c.                                         
    This is working as designed. If users wish to possibly have MIDI            
    support they need to contact the respective manufacturer for                
    these drivers.
