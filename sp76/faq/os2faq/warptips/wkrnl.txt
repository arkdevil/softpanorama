TITLE: 950116 INSTALLING WARP AFTER THE FIRST PHASE, GET ERROR
              MESSAGE " OS2KRNL IS NOT ACCEPTABLE ... "

DESCRIPTION OF SYMPTOMS:
 OS2KRNL is unacceptable on the first reboot when installing WARP on
 system with a Micropolis 2112mb SCSI drive.  It is connected to a
 Future Domain card.

RESOLUTION:
 Users need to contact Micropolis for assistance getting an upgrade ROM
 for this 2gb drive to work under OS/2 WARP
