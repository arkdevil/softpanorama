TITLE: 941216 SYS02025 SYS02027 ON THE FIRST REBOOT OF INSTALLATION
              OF WARP ON A SYSTEM WITH LOCAL BUS IDE CONTROLLER
              CARD

DESCRIPTION OF SYMPTOMS:
  Installation of WARP, after the first reboot received the SYS02025
  and SYS02027 on a Non-IBM systems with a Vesa Local Bus (VLB) IDE
  controller card.

RESOLUTION:
  Please follow page 178 and 179 of the Guide to OS/2 version 3 manual.
  If the system has a local bus IDE controller card, DISABLE the BIOS
  on the 32-bit local bus IDE controller card.  Check the system manual
  for these instructions.
