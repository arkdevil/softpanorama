What is EBIOS.VXD?

I see EBIOS.VXD mentioned as "LoadFailed" in BOOTLOG.TXT. Is there anything wrong with Windows 95 ?

Most likely everything is just fine. EBIOS.VXD is only used for the extended BIOS on IBM PS/2 computers. Unless you have a PS/2 computer it is normal that EBIOS fails to load.
