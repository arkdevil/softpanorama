  REGISTRATION FEE 

 If you are a NEW RBckLib USER, the registration fee is :

    DELIVERY BY E-MAIL : US $ 25
    DELIVERY BY POST   : US $ 30


  UPGRADE OFFER 

 If you are a REGISTERED USER of RBCKLIB v1.x, there is a special
 upgrade offer :

    DELIVERY BY E-MAIL : US $  5
    DELIVERY BY POST   : US $ 10


  PAYMENT 

 Complete the registration form (RBckLib.REG, Dutch.REG for Dutch users) and
 send it with your payment to the address as mentioned above.

 There are three ways to pay :

 1) CASH - this is the easiest, quickest and therefore PREFERRED method

 2) INTERNATIONAL POSTAGE MONEY ORDER written out in DUTCH FLORINS (DFL)
    You can get the money order at your local post office.
    IMPORTANT : the money order should be written out in DUTCH FLORINS
    and NOT in US DOLLARS nor other currencies !

 3) BANK CHECK, made payable to the author of RBckLib
    IMPORTANT : If you pay using a bank check, you have to ADD USD 10 to
    the registration fee for transfer costs !

 NOTE: CREDIT CARDS are NOT accepted (sorry for that ...)
