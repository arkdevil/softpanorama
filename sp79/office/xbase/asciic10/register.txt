REGISTRATION

This is a shareware product.  You may evaluate it for 30 days.  
If you like the product after the 30-day evaluation period,
and you continue to use it, please register and pay the 
registration fee. 

Registered users will receive a 3.5 inch master diskette, a
printed User Guide and an official Registration Certificate.
Registered users will receive lifetime support and are entitled 
to free software upgrades during the first year.

To register, please choose 'Registration, Copyright' from the
applications' About menu.  The instructions are shown on the screen.  
If you register via CompuServe SWREG please use the Registration
ID shown in the 'Registration, Copyright' window.  To register
directly with Informatik Inc, press on 'Print Registration Form',
complete the printed Registration Form and return it to 
Informatik Inc. with your payment.

The registration fees and shiping and handling charges are payable 
in US dollars.  If you wish to pay in Canadian Dollars, please
convert the fees, plus $2.00 bank charges, to Canadian Dollars at 
the current exchange rate.  We also accept checks in other
convertible currencies at the US dollar equivalent, but $3.00 must
be added for bank charges.  If you cannot obtain convertible 
currencies, please contact Informatik Inc. via CompuServe 76534,1166 
or Internet info@informatik.com.

To pay by credit cards, please contact Informatik Inc for detail.
 
We hope that you will register.  Please support the shareware
concept!
