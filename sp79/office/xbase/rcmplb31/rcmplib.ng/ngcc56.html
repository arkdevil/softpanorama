 How to register RCmpLib v3.1
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

 RCmpLib is a SHAREWARE product. It is available world-wide through
 Users Groups, Bulletin Boards, FTP-Servers, etc.

 You have the opportunity to try the program 30 days at no charge to see
 if it meets your needs. If you continue to use the program after
 evaluation, please complete the included registration form
 (RCmpLib.REG, for DUTCH users Dutch.REG) and send it to:

  REGISTRATION FEE 

 If you are a NEW RCMPLIB USER, the registration fee is :

    DELIVERY BY E-MAIL : US $ 25
    DELIVERY BY POST   : US $ 30


  UPGRADE OFFER 

 If you already are a REGISTERED USER of RCmpLib v2.x, the upgrade costs are :

    DELIVERY BY E-MAIL : US $ 10
    DELIVERY BY POST   : US $ 15

 If you already are a REGISTERED USER of RCmpLib v3.x, the upgrade costs are :

    DELIVERY BY E-MAIL : US $  5
    DELIVERY BY POST   : US $ 10


  PAYMENT 

 Just include the appropriate amount of money (IN CASH !!) with the
 registration form (RCmpLib.REG, for DUTCH users Dutch.REG)
 and send it to the address as mentioned above.

 There are three ways to pay :

 1) CASH - this is the easiest, quickest and therefore preferred method

 2) INTERNATIONAL POSTAGE MONEY ORDER written out in DUTCH FLORINS (DFL)
    You can get the money order at your local post office.
    IMPORTANT : the money order should be written out in DUTCH FLORINS
    and NOT in US dollars nor other currencies !

 3) BANK CHECK, made payable to the author of RCmpLib
    IMPORTANT : If you pay using a bank check, you have to add USD 10 to
    the registration fee for transfer costs !

 NOTE: CREDIT CARDS are NOT accepted (sorry for that ...)


  BENEFITS OF REGISTRATION

    by POST.
