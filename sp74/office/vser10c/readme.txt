
Visible::Serialization(tm)
--------------------------
developers' serialization utilities


append a serial number (or other identifier) to an .exe


v1.0c


a product of RCCO Research



Installation
-------------
The program files for Visible::Serialization are provided in full herewith.
Simply copy all files to a hard disk directory of your choosing.  Please
peruse the documentation before attempting to evaluate the utilities in this
package.
