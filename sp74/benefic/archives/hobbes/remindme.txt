RemindMe is a calendar, scheduling and reminder application for OS/2 2.x
and Warp.  Fully customizable and very flexible.  Allows scheduling of 
events and provides audio and/or visual reminders as the events draw near.  
Also comes with an intelligent to-do list which manages events and tasks
you may need to complete.
