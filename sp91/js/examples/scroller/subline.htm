Javascript can improve your life and help you on your journey to fulfillment -- and you don't have to go to therapy, meditate, or even think coherently. Enter your favorite affirmation or suggestion in the text box, and press "Begin". Your message will flash in the statusbar once per second for 25 milliseconds, just at the threshhold of subliminal perception. The message is masked by "white noise" -- garbage text that obscures your conscious perception, but allows the subliminal to be presented directly to the subconscious.
